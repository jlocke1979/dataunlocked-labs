


About Reference Dictionary: 
The informations in these folders comes from this link: 
Source: https://hrsa.unos.org/data/view-data-reports/build-advanced/?category=1%3BDonor

The document is designed for reproducability to document 
the various options selects to produce each report. 
These reports can be found in data/raw/ 
Each rpt is titled "Rpt1.1_...." for easy tying back to this dictionary 



The First digit in the title (e.g. 1.0) represents the 4 sections of reports, organized as follows:
1. Donors 
2. Waitlist
3. Transplants
4. Multiple Transplants

The second digit in the title (eg. 0.1) conotates variations within those groups. 
Most of the variations include Organs (or Multi-Organs) as one of the dimensions (either a row or column)
The other fields are cycled throug paired with section and organ (example, age of organ recipient,). This is repeated for as many of the 'interesting variables' 

Which Varaibles Matter most and got into this dataset?
The reports below likely represent about 80% of the combinations between Organs and some other variable. 
the 20% are the variable I find too complex to manage or not interesting enough to matter.
