


About Reference Dictionary: 
The informations in these folders comes from this link: 
Source: https://hrsa.unos.org/data/view-data-reports/build-advanced/?category=1%3BDonor

The document is designed for reproducability to document 
the various options selects to produce each report. 
These reports can be found in data/raw/ 
Each rpt is titled "Rpt1.1_...." for easy tying back to this dictionary 


The First digit in the title (e.g. 1.0) represents the 4 sections of reports, organized as follows:
1.X.X Waitlists
2.X.X Donors 
3.X.X Transplants
4.X.X Multi-Transplants


The second digit in the title (eg. 0.1) conotates variations within those groups. 
Most of the variations include Location related data 
X.1.X - Donation Service Area
x.2.x - Transplant Center  - (Waitlist Assosciated)
X.3.X - State 
x.4.X - Region 


Other secondar variables of interest are typically
X.X.1 - Organs - (applies to all)
X.X.2 - Organs discarded
X.X.3 - Organs recovered
X.X.4 - Organs Transplanted -
X.X.5 - Diagnosis Category / Daignosis 
X.X.6 - Cause of Death / Circumstance of Death / Mechanism of Death 





All Reports Have these Features In common - Do not alter acre
Year = 2025


The report Number System (Examples): 
Rpt 1.1.1 =  Waitlist_DonationServiceArea_Organs
Rpt 1.1.2 =  Waitlist_DonationServiceArea_Organs Recovered
...
Rpt 4.3.3 =  MultiTransplants_Region_OrgansTransplanted
