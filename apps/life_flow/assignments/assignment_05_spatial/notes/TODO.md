# Document Color Pallett

ALL organs — #3F4750 — deep charcoal slate; infrastructural/system-wide anchor color

Heart — #9A4C54 — muted cardiac burgundy; emotionally resonant without oversaturated medical red

Liver — #B07A3B — warm amber/ochre; earthy metabolic association

Lung — #6E92A8 — airy steel blue; breathing/air association

Kidney — #4F8A83 — teal-green; filtration/water association

Pancreas — #7A668F — muted plum; distinct but subdued specialty color

Kidney-Pancreas — #3E6B73 — darker blue-teal; related to kidney but perceptually distinct

Liver-Kidney — #8B6B3F — earthy brown-gold; related to liver but darker/heavier

Intestine / GI — #7C8561 — muted olive; organic digestive association

Multi-organ other — #5B6675 — cool slate indigo; complex/specialized category

Design notes:

- Palette designed for muted editorial aesthetics, not dashboard saturation.

- Colors chosen to preserve distinction under deuteranopia, protanopia, and tritanopia as much as practical.

- Color functions as a supplementary cue, not the primary carrier of meaning.

- Geography, layout, labels, and small multiples should carry the main analytical load.

- Dense overlapping network maps should avoid excessive categorical saturation.
