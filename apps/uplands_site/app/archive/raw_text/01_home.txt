The Uplands Peoria
The Uplands is a neighborhood founded in 1902 in the heart of Peoria, Illinois. We take pride in our historic homes, tree-lined boulevards, diversity of residents, and close-knit community. Within easy walking distance of our homes are a supermarket, a pharmacy, an Asian market, and several unique restaurants. We also enjoy walking to events at Bradley University and playing in Bradley Park. The Uplands Residential Association (URA) is open to all neighborhood residents and is a very strong and active organization.

The URA publishes a monthly newsletter with important information for the neighborhood. View the latest Uplanders.
