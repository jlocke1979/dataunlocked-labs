Schools
The public schools serving our neighborhood are Whittier Primary School (K-4), Calvin Coolidge (5-8), and Peoria (Central) High School (9-12). Students in grades 5 – 8 meeting entrance criteria may choose to attend Washington Gifted School or Roosevelt Magnet School (Fine Arts Magnet). Peoria District 150 also has some fine Early Childhood Education programs for children ages 3 to 6. Even if you do not have children in our neighborhood schools, consider volunteering in one of the schools. Healthy public schools make healthy neighborhoods!
