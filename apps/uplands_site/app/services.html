Resident Services

The Uplands Residential Association coordinates a small set of practical services for neighborhood residents. This page summarizes what is typically available; exact dates, coverage, and contacts are confirmed by the board each season.

Snow Removal

Neighborhood sidewalk snow removal is one of the association’s core resident services. When funded for the season, the URA arranges contracted clearing of public sidewalks in the Uplands after qualifying snow events — so pedestrians can move through the neighborhood more safely in winter.

Service details — including the contractor, fund status, and exact coverage map — are reviewed and confirmed by the board before each winter. Check the monthly Uplander newsletter or contact a board officer if you are unsure what applies for the current season.

What residents may want to know

  • Service area: Public sidewalks within the Uplands, as defined by the board for the current contract. Private walks and driveways are generally not included. Exact streets and boundaries to be confirmed by the board.
  • Timing and trigger conditions: Clearing typically begins after a snowfall that meets the season’s accumulation threshold, and after city street plows have finished in the neighborhood. Threshold amount and scheduling details to be confirmed by the board.
  • Contact and questions: Report missed areas or other concerns to a URA officer — not directly to the contractor. Officer contacts appear in the Uplander and on the Contact page. Current-season reporting contact to be confirmed by the board.
  • Winter reminders: Snow may be plowed from sidewalks into adjacent yards. Residents who are able may still wish to keep steps and private walks clear. Contributions to the snow removal fund are accepted when the board opens giving for the season.

Annual Garage Sale

The neighborhood garage sale is a recurring community event. Participating households sell from their own properties on a shared date, and the URA publishes a map to help shoppers find sales across the Uplands.

The event is organized by a volunteer committee. Registration, maps, and final logistics are posted here when details are ready for the current year.

Event details

  • Date: To be posted — traditionally the Saturday before Mother’s Day.
  • Registration: Link or instructions to be posted before the event.
  • Map and participating homes: Current-year map and list of participating addresses to be posted here.
  • Contact: Garage sale committee chair to be confirmed by the board.
