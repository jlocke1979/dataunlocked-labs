Why the Snow Fund Matters

When the fund is fully subscribed, the URA hires a contractor to clear public sidewalks across the Uplands after qualifying snow events — so neighbors, students, and visitors can walk safely through the neighborhood all winter.

  • Clearing begins after snowfall meets the season threshold and city plows finish on Uplands streets.
  • Contracted clearing covers association-defined public walks — not private driveways or steps.
  • Equal coverage across the neighborhood helps pedestrians of every age and ability.

Fund status and service details are confirmed by the board each fall. Watch the Uplander newsletter or contact a board officer with questions.
