Support the Uplands

Neighbors pitch in each year to keep the association running. Contributions are voluntary — suggested amounts, not required fees.

Annual Recurring Support

Prefer to make your neighborhood support automatic? Annual recurring support helps the Uplands plan ahead for snow removal, beautification, communications, and community projects.

Before completing payment, please confirm the amount and billing frequency shown by PayPal. Recurring contributions can be managed or canceled through PayPal.

Payments are handled by PayPal. The Uplands website does not collect or store payment information.

One-Time Contribution

One-time contributions can be used for Annual Membership, Snow Fund, Beautification Fund, or general association support. Note which fund you are supporting in the PayPal message field.

Payments are handled by PayPal. The Uplands website does not collect or store payment information.

Where Your Support Goes

Snow Removal

Winter sidewalk clearing so neighbors can move safely through the Uplands.

Beautification & Gardening

Boulevard plantings, native restoration, and seasonal neighborhood care.

Community Communications & Events

The Uplander, meetings, picnics, and activities that keep us connected.

Questions about suggested amounts or how the association is funded? See the Association Overview.
